Parking

How To Add Parking

  • After the user has create his/her account, they will click add on add parking which navigates them to another web page. The user will then enter the following information accepted by the app - Building numerical code - Number of hours - The user’s vehicle car plate number - Number of hosts The app will then check and notify the user if their vehicle car plate number is the same as when they first entered the number as per sign up. If both data match, then the app will add user’s parking monthly and charge the user. The first three parking’s are free of charge in every month, afterwards, there is a cost of: - $4 per hour or less - $8 for up to 3 hours - $12 for up to 3 hours - $12 for up to 10 hours - $20 for up to 24 hours. The Parking app will then verify the user’s information, all parking information with additional information, such as, date and time of parking and saves it under their account upon clicking confirm parking.

Parking Receipt

  • After the user had completed adding the parking and clicked Confirm Parking, the user is navigated to the receipt information which displays user’s car plate number, date and time of the parking, duration of parking, numerical building code, suit # of host and the parking cost for the latest parking that the user has added. Upon selecting an item of receipt information, the app will provide list of the previous parking done as well as displaying the specific details of the previous parking information.
