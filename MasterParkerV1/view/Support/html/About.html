About

  • Master Parker is mobile app that essentially allows the customers to park their car. From this, customers can add new parking and locate the specific parking locations using google maps and update the vehicle information under their account name. To add their cars to the parking app, user has to first create and account, enter the vehicle information and the method of payment. The charges will be set as per the total duration of time the car has been parked. For conformation, the user will give a receipt with the list of current and previous user and vehicle information. The app also provides customer support through email and call extensions.
